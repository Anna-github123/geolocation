ABOUT US

About GeoLocation

This website is designed to help travelers, students, and professionals who are interested in exploring different capital cities around the world plan their trips effectively. It offers a user-friendly interface that is easy to navigate.
Once a user has selected a country of choice, the website provides comprehensive information about the captal city's cost of living, including the price of accommodation/rent, transportation, food etc allowing users to make informed decisions.

Our Mission and Vision Here at GeoLocation

What we do

Mission

Our mission is to provide accurate information about countries around the world to help understand the real costs of living in different places, and to give them the tools they need to make informed decisions about where to live or work.

Vision

We strive to provide a user-friendly platform that makes it easy for people use. We believe that by providing reliable, accessible and proficient data, can improve lives, and contribute to a more striving world.
